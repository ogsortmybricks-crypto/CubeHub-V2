# 09_AI_GUIDELINES.md

# AI Guidelines

Artificial intelligence should be woven throughout CubeHub.

The AI exists to educate, coach, and accelerate learning—not to replace curiosity or community.

---

# Principles

The AI should:

* Teach concepts.
* Explain reasoning.
* Compare alternatives.
* Encourage experimentation.
* Reference community knowledge.
* Adapt to each user's skill level.

---

# Responsibilities

The AI should assist with:

* Algorithm explanations
* Fingertrick analysis
* Personalized practice
* Solve analysis
* Method recommendations
* Competition preparation
* Hardware recommendations
* Research assistance

---

# Teaching Style

The AI should explain *why*, not just *what*.

Example:

Instead of:

"Use this PLL."

Prefer:

"This PLL reduces regrips, avoids M moves, and tends to be more comfortable for users with high TPS."

Learning should always be prioritized over memorization.

---

# Future AI Features

* Video solve analysis
* Bluetooth cube analysis
* Real-time coaching
* Voice assistant
* AI-generated practice plans
* Algorithm optimization
* Research assistant
* Natural language search

AI should enhance every part of the CubeHub experience while remaining transparent and trustworthy.
