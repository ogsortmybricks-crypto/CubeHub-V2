# CubeHub Vision

> **The Operating System of Speedcubing**

---

## Our Vision

CubeHub exists to become the digital home of speedcubing.

Not another timer.

Not another algorithm database.

Not another forum.

CubeHub is where every aspect of speedcubing comes together into one connected ecosystem.

Learning.

Practice.

Competition.

Research.

Community.

History.

Innovation.

If someone discovers speedcubing today, CubeHub should be the platform they continue using for years.

If someone develops a revolutionary solving method, CubeHub should be where it is published.

If someone builds a cubing application, CubeHub should provide the infrastructure.

If someone wants to understand the history of the sport, CubeHub should preserve it.

CubeHub is not simply software.

It is infrastructure for the future of speedcubing.

---

## The Future

Imagine opening CubeHub every morning.

You complete your daily practice.

Your AI coach recommends what to train.

A favorite creator has published a new algorithm.

A competition you registered for starts next week.

Your club is discussing a new method.

A new world record reconstruction has been uploaded.

Everything you care about as a speedcuber exists in one place.

That is the future CubeHub is being built to create.

---

## Our Goal

Become the platform every speedcuber opens every day.

Become the place where the community builds the future of speedcubing.

Become the operating system of speedcubing.
