# 10_FUTURE_VISION.md

# Future Vision

CubeHub should be designed not only for today's cubing community, but for the next decade of innovation.

---

# Long-Term Goals

Become the operating system of speedcubing.

Become the world's largest archive of cubing knowledge.

Become the primary platform for learning, collaboration, and research.

Provide the infrastructure that powers the next generation of cubing software.

---

# Emerging Technologies

Future versions of CubeHub may include:

* Smart cube integration
* AI solve reconstruction
* Eye-tracking analytics
* Augmented reality trainers
* Virtual reality practice environments
* Live AI coaching during training
* Robotics integration
* Advanced performance analytics

---

# CubeHub Labs

Create a space for experimental ideas.

Examples:

* New solving methods
* Algorithm optimization research
* Community experiments
* AI prototypes
* Educational tools

Successful experiments can graduate into the core platform.

---

# Open Ecosystem

CubeHub should become the platform other cubing projects build upon.

Support:

* Plugins
* Extensions
* Public APIs
* Developer SDKs
* Community-built tools

Innovation should come from the community as much as the core team.

---

# Legacy

The ultimate success of CubeHub is not measured by user count or revenue.

It is measured by whether the speedcubing community is stronger because CubeHub exists.

If future cubers learn faster, collaborate more effectively, preserve their history, and continue advancing the sport through CubeHub, then the mission has been accomplished.
